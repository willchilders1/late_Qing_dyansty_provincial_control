Workforce and Economy in California

California's work force is one of the largest in the world, only smaller in population than 34 countries. San Diego, San Francisco, Los Angeles, and San Jose are all in the top 15 Populous cities in the United States. Additionally, the state also boasts one of the world's largest economy, with the 2019 Gross State Product being the largest in the U.S. at 3.2 Trillion dollars. Trade and agriculture are both integral to the success of the state, with much of the northern portion dedicated to many agricultural activities and the south a booming commerical center. Because the state is extremely large and geographically diverse there is a disparity in the wealth of employed individuals, with those in agricultural areas making far less than those living in San Franscisco, Los Angeles, etc.
