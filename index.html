Rise and Fall of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom: A Timeline

Timeline: 1850-1864

The Taiping Rebellion cost between 20 and 70 million lives, making it one of the deadliest conflicts in human history. Though underrepresented in modern historical discourse, this rebellion signalled the death knell of the Qing Empire and Imperial China writ large.
